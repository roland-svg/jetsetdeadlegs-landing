3. The data we collect

The data we collect depends on how you use JetSet Dead Legs. We aim to collect only what we need to operate, improve, and secure the Service.

3.1 Data you provide to us directly

Traveller accounts and usage

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account login details (such as password, stored in hashed form)
  • Route preferences and alerts (for example, origin, destination, dates, and time windows)
  • Enquiries and messages you send through the platform or in response to notifications
  • Any additional details you choose to share about your flight preferences or requirements

Operator accounts and usage

  • Operator or company name and contact details
  • Business contact person’s name, email address, and phone number
  • Operator account login details
  • Fleet and aircraft details (type, registration, configurations)
  • Flight leg listings, pricing, and availability
  • Company information required for verification (such as AOC details, insurance information, and registration numbers)
  • Billing and payout details as required by our payment provider (Stripe), including information submitted directly to Stripe

Verification documentation

To verify operators and support trust and safety, we may collect or receive:

  • Air Operator Certificate (AOC) documentation
  • Insurance certificates
  • Aircraft registration and ownership documents
  • Business registration or identity documentation, where required by law or our risk framework

These documents are stored using secure infrastructure and used to support our verification checks and trust badges.

Communications

When you contact us (for example by email, contact form, or in‑app messaging), we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The content of your message and any attachments
  • Meta‑data about the interaction (such as time and channel)

3.2 Data we collect automatically

When you use our website or app, we may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed, and referring URLs)
  • Basic analytics events, such as which screens or pages you visit, which buttons you click, and how long you spend in different parts of the Service
  • PWA‑related information, such as whether you installed the app to your device

We use this information to keep the Service secure, diagnose problems, and understand how people use JetSet Dead Legs so we can improve it.

3.3 Data from third parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as:

  • Payment provider (Stripe): transaction status, charges, refunds, dispute and fraud information, and payout status. We do not store or see full card numbers.
  • Verification or compliance partners: checks relating to operator legitimacy, sanctions screening, or fraud prevention (if such services are used).
  • Analytics and error tracking tools: aggregated or pseudonymised data about app performance and usage.
