Monetize Your Empty Legs

JetSet Dead Legs helps AOC-certified operators turn ferry flights into revenue. Reach a new audience of savvy travelers while maintaining full operational control.

Why operators use JetSet Dead Legs

Traditional empty-leg marketing is fragmented:

  • Ad-hoc website listings and social posts
  • Email blasts that go unread
  • Manual replies to low-quality enquiries

JetSet Dead Legs gives you:

  • A focused marketplace for empty legs and repositioning flights
  • Travellers who understand that empty legs are opportunistic, not on-demand charter
  • A simple way to test demand on new routes without rebuilding your tech stack

You decide what to list, when, and at what price.

How it works for operators

1

Onboard as an AOC-verified operator

Share your AOC details, operating company, and coverage regions. Confirm insurance and basic profile information.

2

Publish empty legs and ferry flights

List sectors you are already planning to fly. Highlight key routes, dates, and approximate timings. Indicate aircraft type and typical configuration.

3

Receive qualified enquiries

Travellers see clearly that these are empty-leg opportunities with defined constraints. You receive structured enquiries rather than random emails and calls.

4

Confirm and get paid through the platform

You confirm availability, timing, and price. Payment is processed via Stripe and released after the flight, minus platform fees.

You keep full control over aircraft, crew, and operational decisions at all times.

Benefits for operators

With JetSet Dead Legs you can:

Monetise repositioning

Earn incremental revenue on flights that would otherwise go empty.

Protect your brand and workflow

Keep your own dispatch, ops, and safety processes. Use JetSet Dead Legs as a demand and revenue layer, not a replacement system.

See where demand really is

Identify which routes, dates, and airports generate the most engagement. Use those insights to inform future schedules and pricing.

Offer a better traveller experience

Clear information on who is operating the flight. Simple, secure booking and payment flow. A consistent message around safety and AOC verification.

Safety, compliance, and your AOC

We built JetSet Dead Legs around the reality that:

  • Safety and compliance sit with the operator, not the software.
  • AOC-certified operators already have robust systems and oversight in place.

On the platform:

  • We highlight that flights are operated by independent, AOC-certified operators.
  • We make your role clear to travellers: you are the operator of the flight, not JetSet Dead Legs.
  • We align messaging with your regulatory obligations, not against them.

Our Safety & AOC-verified operators page gives travellers a plain-English explanation of how this works.
