Major Milestone January 2026

Soft Rollout Complete, Private Beta Launching

JetSet Dead Legs has successfully completed its soft rollout phase and is now launching a focused private beta with selected AOC-verified operators across the UK and Europe.

What We've Shipped

  • ✓ Full marketplace platform with AI-powered pricing suggestions
  • ✓ Automated AOC verification system cross-referencing CAA and EASA databases
  • ✓ Stripe Connect payment infrastructure with escrow for secure transactions
  • ✓ Mobile and web applications for both travelers and operators

Private Beta Goals

  • • Onboard 15–18 quality operators within 60 days
  • • Reach 50+ monthly empty-leg listings
  • • Prove 15–20% fill rate on listed empty legs
  • • Validate pricing models and route demand patterns

What This Means for Operators

During private beta, selected AOC-verified operators receive:

  • • Charter Pro subscription free for 90 days
  • • Beta commission rate of just 3–5% (vs. traditional broker rates of 10–15%)
  • • AI-powered pricing suggestions based on route and demand data
  • • Automated empty leg publishing and traveler matching
  • • Secure payouts 24–48 hours after flight completion

What This Means for Travelers

Private beta provides access to:

  • • Verified empty legs from AOC-certified operators
  • • Focus on key UK–EU routes: London–Palma, Farnborough–Nice, Biggin Hill–Geneva, Luton–Olbia
  • • Transparent pricing with no hidden broker markups
  • • Secure booking and payment through escrow system
  • • Direct connection with licensed operators (we are a platform, not a broker)

Next Steps: We're inviting operators to join private beta in batches to ensure quality onboarding and platform stability. Travelers can access the platform immediately to browse available empty legs from verified operators.
